Data & Privacy ▾
This application does not collect, store, or transmit your message content. Communication style data — consisting solely of aggregated emotional tone indicators — is stored locally on your device and is not shared with any third party. Each message you send is processed by a third-party translation engine solely for the purpose of translation. No historical message data is transmitted at any time. All locally stored preference data remains on your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser or app storage.
